Mensagens da Pastora

“When you pray, heaven moves. Never underestimate the power of a sincere prayer.”

“The Holy Spirit is our counselor, comforter, and guide. Listen to His voice and walk with confidence.”

“No matter the size of the storm, our God is still greater.”

“God's grace not only reaches us, it transforms us.”

“Be light where there is darkness. You were called to shine.”

“Communion with God is the fuel of the soul. Seek Him daily.”
